The Child and Nature Alliance of Canada supports deep and meaningful connections to nature and the outdoors to foster healthy childhood development and transformative learning experiences for Canadian children and youth. We are invested in collaborative leadership and a multi-sector approach to further innovative practice, policy development and research.

Forest School Canada [FSC] is an education program of the Child and Nature Alliance of Canada. The goal of FSC is to promote and support the Forest and Nature School movement across Canada, mobilizing communities of practice at regional and national levels.

For more information, visit the Child and Nature Alliance of Canada and Forest School Canada websites.
